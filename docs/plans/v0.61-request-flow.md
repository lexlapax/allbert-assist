## Main User/Operator Flows

- An operator navigates an app whose information architecture and navigation were
  redesigned in v0.60 and implemented here: the redesigned shell and navigation
  place workspace, jobs, objectives, settings, and the operator panels per the
  v0.60 IA model.
- A visitor lands on a real `/` marketing surface with the Allbert brand,
  SEO/OG metadata, and variant-registry CTAs, composed per the v0.60 IA, then
  enters the app shell.
- An operator works in a redesigned shell carrying the Allbert logo/wordmark
  instead of the stock Phoenix asset.
- An operator sees entrance/drawer/skeleton motion that collapses to instant
  when their reduced-motion preference is set.
- An operator reads the redesigned `/workspace`, `/jobs`, `/objectives`, and
  operator panels with real visual hierarchy and populated empty/first-run
  states.
- A first-run operator on an empty workspace sees "what can I do"
  suggested-action affordances shaped by the v0.60 onboarding-flow design and
  First-Model Path (view-only; seats the v0.63 guided-onboarding wizard).
- An operator with `system` theme selected gets a dark theme resolved from the
  OS `prefers-color-scheme` preference.

## Data Flow

The IA/navigation redesign, redesigned screens, brand assets, motion, hierarchy,
dark-mode resolution, and SEO/OG metadata are presentation concerns rendered
through the unified catalog inside one redesigned app shell. The structural
redesign recomposes screens per the v0.60 information architecture through the
existing catalog/shell/token substrate — it adds no new rendering path and no new
data-layer path; the same DTOs render through redesigned screens. The walking
skeleton from v0.60 becomes the real navigation frame the redesigned screens
fill, built by extending the variant registry and pattern library per the v0.60
design-system gap analysis. The landing surface emits the shell data contract,
consumes tokens, and uses the variant registry. Motion durations/easings come
from the v0.58 motion-scale tokens; the reduced-motion axis gates them. Dark mode
resolves app tokens from the operator's theme setting plus OS
`prefers-color-scheme`. Suggested-action affordances and landing CTAs compose
registered-action DTOs (ADR 0073) for display only, along the v0.60 onboarding-
flow path; no backend, model, or data-layer path is added.

## Authority Invariants

No new capability and no authority change. The catalog stays the rendering
boundary (ADR 0030/0074). The IA/navigation redesign, redesigned screens, brand,
motion, hierarchy, landing, dark mode, and suggested-action affordances are
presentation-only — the structural redesign recomposes screens through the
existing substrate and grants no authority. Suggested actions and landing CTAs
render registered-action DTOs and grant no authority or egress; launcher
selection stays view-only. No model- or data-generated markup. No new route
beyond the landing surface. No rebuild of the rendering or data substrate
(tokens/registry/pattern-library/catalog/shell and canvas/tiles/ephemerals/
Fragment-HMAC/persistence are reused as-is). No internal rename. SEO/OG metadata
is static and exposes no operator data or secrets.
