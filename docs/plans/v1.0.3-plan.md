## Purpose

Global-state ownership is the primary shared cause of the convertible serial
floor and the two surviving composition failures; intentional security-eval
and genuine external-runtime floors remain distinct. Phase 1 (v1.0.2) made
the lanes HONEST (reconciliation 173→0, residue de-flaked, web split,
cost-packed partitions, decide-turn remediation) but converted only a
handful of tests. Phase 2 does the actual migration: convert the measured
serial floor to async where an ownership seam exists, retire the two
monolith-only failure classes at their global-state root, and take the last
attributed decide-turn hot path.

The acceptance bar (AGENTS.md: Purpose is the bar): measured lane-floor
reduction with ZERO test-feature loss — every conversion proves identical
test identities/totals and a reviewed assertion-body diff; the 20-seed
monolith campaign rerun
shows both residual classes at 0; `release.v1` + `release.v103` green; the
version's own binary validated under the standing 1.x release policy.
